Our Story

From a small South Side shop to a coastal Georgia destination

Ryan Mitchell founded Ryan's Motorcycle Sales in 2008 after spending 10 years riding, maintaining, and learning every detail he could about motorcycles. What began as a passion project quickly became a trusted place for riders who wanted honesty, skill, and a dealership that truly understood the road.

The business started with a modest shop on the south side of Savannah, serving local riders who valued straightforward advice and dependable machines. As word spread, the dealership expanded to meet demand from across coastal Georgia, building a reputation for quality inventory, careful service, and lasting relationships.

Today, Ryan's Motorcycle Sales helps riders find the right bike, keep it performing at its best, and enjoy the kind of dealership experience that feels personal from the first visit. With over 500 bikes sold annually, the shop continues to grow without losing the hands-on service that built its name.

What drives us

  • Friendly guidance for first-time buyers and seasoned riders alike.
  • Inventory selected for value, performance, and rider confidence.
  • Service rooted in experience, transparency, and long-term trust.
